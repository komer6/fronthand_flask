About Us

The Library Management System is designed to help manage the borrowing and returning of books in a library. Our goal is to provide a user-friendly interface that streamlines the process for both staff and patrons.

Features:

  • Manage books: Add, view, and delete books in the library.
  • Manage customers: Add and view customer details.
  • Track loans: Keep track of which books are loaned out and their return dates.
  • Check for late loans: Identify which loans are overdue.
